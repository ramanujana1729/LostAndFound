About Us

'Lost and Found Mumbai' is an initiative by Abhinavan(We Can We Will) Foundation. We are a group of youngsters willing to help the society in all possible ways. One of the initiatives is 'Lost and Found Mumbai' wherein if you've lost or found something in Mumbai, you can contact us & we can connect you with the other person.

People become so stressed if they cannot find their object. You have to run behind the government offiials or RTO or bank if you have lost your wallet. Our mission is to help return lost items to their rightful owners. We look forward to a future where people could live in a world they need not stress if they have lost something because we are just a click away!

HOW IT WORKS?

For example, if John has lost his wallet then he will first check if someone has found it and uploaded it on our website. if not, then he will upload all the details in the lost section of our website. We will post this information on our social media handles so that if anyone finds it, they can contact us.

Suppose Joe finds John's wallet, then she will upload the details in the found section of our website. If the details match, we will contact John. For verification purpose, John will upload proof of his item and valid ID to claim his lost wallet. Once verified John can collect his wallet from the pick up location.
